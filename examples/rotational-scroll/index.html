Inhaler

The Inhaler was designed for performing
herbal inhalations at home. It was made of

resistant to heat and water.

Natural medicine treatments ceramic set.

The project explores the possibilities of alternative ways of healing in modern world, based on European and ayurvedic water and temperature-based methods.

Natural therapies have originated almost 10,000 years ago and has continued to evolve since then. Nowadays we tend to forget about them, in the first place reaching for synthetic pharmaceutical products that often cause side effects.
